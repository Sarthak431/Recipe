Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredients
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon instant yeast
1 teaspoon sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1 cup warm water
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 cup pizza sauce
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
Assorted toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, bell peppers)

Instructions

1 In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, yeast, sugar, and salt.
2 Add the warm water and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.
3 Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes, until it becomes smooth and elastic.
4 Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a clean kitchen towel, and let it rise for 1 hour.
5 Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).
6 Punch down the dough and divide it into two equal portions.
7 Roll out each portion into a round shape to fit your pizza pan.
8 Place the dough on the pizza pan and spread pizza sauce over the surface.
9 Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
10 Add your preferred toppings (e.g., pepperoni, mushrooms, onions, bell peppers).
11 Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
12 Remove from the oven, slice, and serve hot.

Enjoy your homemade pizza!
